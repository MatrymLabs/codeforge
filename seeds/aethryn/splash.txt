                 ~ The Kindlands Coast ~

 A world that was Forged from ember, and Unforged once.
 You wash ashore a Forger: you can hold ember and give
 it lasting shape. The town forge has just relit. The
 road north runs to Emberreach, the Warden Gate, and the
 Great Spiral beyond. What was made can be remade.

 Relight the first ember. Then prove yourself.
